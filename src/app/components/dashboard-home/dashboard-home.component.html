General Information

There will be 10 tests for the course, each test being worth 10 points, totaling 100 points for tests.

There will be 2 homeworks, each worth 50 points, totaling 100 points for homework.

The final project will be worth 100 points.

Tests weight in final grade: 30%, Homework weight: 30%, Final Project: 40%.

The course will be held every Tuesday at 18:30 and will last 2 hours, with a 20-minute break (1:40 of course + 20 minutes). In the first half, we will learn theory, followed by practice exercises and demonstrations.

Tests will be available each week from the first day of the course, can be taken up to 3 times, with the highest grade recorded. Questions are randomly selected from a pool of 200 questions for each student. Responses will not be shown after the test, but will be discussed in the next course session for better understanding.

We recommend using a webcam for natural discussions and better understanding of expressions, but it's optional if you're in a hurry or just woke up. It's better to attend without a camera than not at all.
